Hi, my name is Laura, and I am creative, which means I like to both code and put colours and fonts together in visual presentation. I also have a passion for animals and their welfare, and would love to help out shelters or any other animal-related establishments.

So what can I do for you and what does this process look like?

For a website, I start with a wireframe with text boxes, squares and other shapes for placement your logo, pictures, and other elements. Next, after that is approved, I will send you pictures of the mockup pages, with your logo and other elements instead of the shapes. After that is approved, I start coding for your website, sending you weekly screenshots as it progresses. For a logo, I start out with sketches from brainstorming after the design brief I send to you to fill out is sent back. If approved, I will colour it with chosen colours from the design brief, and send it to you to see what you think. Again, progress shots will be sent weekly.
